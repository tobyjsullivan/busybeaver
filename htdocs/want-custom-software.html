Improving Your Business

We are commited to your satisfaction and ensuring you end up with the best solution for your business. We accomplish this by involving you in every step of the development process and giving you maximum visibility into our work.

You can expect your project to proceed similar to the following:

  1. Introduction You make initial contact and tell us about the project you have in mind. We will ask some basic questions to gain familiarity with your business and needs. At the end of this, we'll schedule a time to meet for a more in-depth investigation of your needs.
  2. Defining the Project We will meet, either in person or over the phone, to discuss your project in depth. This meeting will flesh out the exact requirements of your business and what the successful system will look like.
  3. Project Proposal After the details have been worked out, we will present you with a detailed project proposal for your review and agreement. Usually this proposal will break down the project into the various different features that address each of your needs and estimate the complexity of implementing each part. You will have the opportunity to review this proposal and prioritise the various options based on your unique business case.
  4. Development Work With an approved proposal, we have everything we need to get to work on your project. As work commenses, we will be able to provide you with regular updates on the project status and also demonstrate completed work at regular intervals to ensure your investment matches your expectations.
  5. Training, Support and Monitoring Our partnership doesn't end with delivery of the finished project. To ensure your project is successful, we will monitor your system for errors and problems. Additionally, we are also willing to provide training to your staff on any systems we build.
